6 Ways To Support Orange Polynomial Band

FIND OUT HOW

Help by: Shopping Our Firehouse Barbecue Fundraiser

We've partnered with Firehouse Barbecue to offer these delicious smoked meats, sauces, and Butcher's Crates at a great price. Every purchase contributes directly to Orange Polynomial's goals.

  • Boston Butt

    Delicious Boston Butts smoked to perfection. Our Boston Butts are premium quality, locally sourced, and slowly hickory smoked over 16 hours until they reach exquisite fork-tenderness.

  • Half Ham

    Our Half Hams are locally sourced and the finest quality available. They are twice-smoked, yielding an incredibly tender texture and delicious flavor you'll only get from Firehouse.

  • Whole Turkey

    Succulent smoked whole turkeys. Our turkeys are sourced from the finest local butchers, then slow-smoked to juicy, crisp-skinned perfection. These birds are blast-frozen immediately after smoking to preserve every bit of its delicious flavor.

IMPORTANT: Don't miss the pickup date!

WHERE:

Orange Polynomial High School Auditorium
123 Anywhere St.
Polynomial, AL 35962

WHEN: 2:00 - 4:00 P.M.

Help by: Sharing far and wide

Sharing this page helps us meet our goals by reaching people that we can't who might be able to contribute toward our mission. Every share helps!

Help by: Making a donation

Want to contribute without buying any barbecue? Fantastic! 100% of donations go directly to Orange Polynomial Band.

Help by: Becoming a volunteer

Willing to offer some hands-on assistance? That's amazing! Let us get to know you a little bit and we'll reach out with how you can help.
